About KNSJ

For 10 years, KNSJ 89.1 FM has been San Diego's leading community-powered, volunteer-run radio station. Launching in 2013 against all ods, we have grown and thrived, amplifying diverse voices and provided locally-focused programming not heard on corporate radio.

This milestone anniversary provides a chance to look back on how far we've come as well as enables us to look ahead to the future.
